Atom

The smallest unit of an element that retains its chemical properties. Consists of a dense nucleus (protons + neutrons) surrounded by orbiting electrons [web:1][web:2].

Molecule

Two or more atoms chemically bonded together, representing the smallest unit of a compound that shows its characteristic properties [web:6][web:20].

Compound

A pure substance formed when two or more different elements chemically combine in fixed proportions by mass [web:7][web:14].

Particle

A minute portion of matter. In chemistry, refers to subatomic particles (electrons, protons, neutrons) or larger matter portions [web:8][web:15].

Electron

Negatively charged subatomic particle orbiting the nucleus in electron shells. Determines chemical bonding and reactivity [web:9][web:16].

Proton & Neutron

Proton: Positively charged particle in nucleus (defines atomic number).
Neutron: Neutral particle in nucleus (adds mass, stabilizes atom) [web:9][web:16].
